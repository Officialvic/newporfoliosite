Meet Me

I'm Victor O. Adeoye, a civil and structural engineer with a passion for sustainable infrastructure and empowering communities. While I design structures by day, my evenings are dedicated to using design thinking to craft user-friendly software and other technological solutions.

Beyond engineering, I'm a firm believer in the power of technology for social good. Partnering with the Nigerian Library Association, I've equipped over 500 professionals and students with media and digital literacy tools to combat misinformation and fake news. My dedication extends to community outreach programs, supporting internally displaced persons and orphans and providing not just resources, but also hope.

This multifaceted approach allows me to bring a unique blend of technical expertise, design thinking, and social responsibility to every project. I'm eager to collaborate on ventures that combine innovation with positive impact.

I am open to opportunities in the following areas: Structural Engineer, Researcher, Product Designer, Product Developer, Capacity Building Facilitator, Program Coordinator, Project Management, or Community Program Coordinator.

I am readily available for a conversation. Please do not hesitate to contact me.
