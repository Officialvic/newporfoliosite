Meet Me

Hello there! My name is Adeoye Victor, and I'm a passionate tech enthusiast who thrives on solving problems in innovative ways. As a team player, I enjoy collaborating with others to analyze and solve problems dynamically and strategically.

In my role as a Web Designer with BRATIM Group, I have had the opportunity to exercise my technical and professional skills to redesign the company's website. By improving its user interaction and experience, we were able to increase their business productivity. With my background in civil engineering and a strong passion for digital literacy advocacy, I have worked with various NGOs to execute several MIL and Digital Literacy projects in both governmental and private sectors.

Currently, I am a fellow in TIIDELab, where I am undergoing intensive capacity building training in software engineering. I am always eager to learn, research, and solve problems innovatively, and I believe that my diverse range of skills and expertise can help individuals and businesses achieve their goals through the use of technology.
